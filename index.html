Campus Life

SCHOOL CHURCH

Was establised in 1975

The chuch was made 12 years after the inaugration of the school at the day Christmas was celebrated. The students go there daily and sing hymns and prayers before the school starts and finish after singing 'We Shall Overcome'. It is important to be puntual and quiet when in church and not cause disturbance, and to sing hymns and prayers when sung.



SCHOOL LIBRARY

Was built in 1970

The library was made 7 years after the school's inaugration at the day when it was founder's day, as a tribute to our founder, Mr. Cabbage, who had worked for over 25 years to make this school. This is a place where you can read any books as you like, to increse your knowledge and to get enjoyment while reading them. Also, you HAVE to be puntual and quient when in library to not disturb the students reading books, which may be related to study or story books. The books here are handpicked by our special book-selectors, and we ensure that story books do not get much boring, or any study books have any wrong information.



SCHOOL AUDITORIUM

Was built in 1965

The auditorium was built 2 years after the inaugration of our school, by our founder, Mr. Cabbage, as a gift to all our students who had worked hard to get good results in exams and their request for builkding an auditorium to showcase their talents and skills onstage. From 1966 onwards, on the Founder's Day and in many such events, our students have been showing their talents and skills on the stage and have been performing brilliantly, and we hope that our auditorium continues toi show more students' talents and skills to the world. You have to be Punctual and Quiet during any function, with the exception of some cases, to ensure no disasters or accidents or disturbances during functions in the auditorium.

Inter-School Science Project Competition
Came 2nd in the inter-school Science Fair.

Our Class VI student, Carrot Parker, won the 2nd prize in our Inter-school Science Fair. He has made a beautiful model of the water cycle, which showed how the water transforms in different states, from evapourate as gas from water bodies, cool down into liquid in the clouds, and fall back as rain. He had also labeled the different processes in the water cycle, like Evapouration, Condensation and Precipitaion. He also used water and various equipments to show how various processes in the water cycle work. We are very proud of his creativity and skills, and we hope he will make great science and other projects in the inter-school science fair and in other inter school tournaments.
